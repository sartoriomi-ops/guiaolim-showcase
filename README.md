## 💡 What it is

GuiaOlim is a content brand that helps Latin American immigrants navigate life in Israel. It covers the practical information that's hard to find in Spanish: bureaucracy, banking, healthcare, housing, and workplace culture. All content is written in clear, jargon-free, neutral Spanish.

---

## 📋 What it covers

| Topic | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Government** | Bituach Leumi (social security), banking setup |
| **Healthcare** | Kupat Cholim enrollment, navigating the system |
| **Housing** | Rental contracts, arnona (municipal tax) |
| **Work** | Workplace culture, Hebrew for the office, job search strategies |
| **Community** | Ulpan tips, social integration resources |

## 💡 Why I built it

The practical information Latin American olim need when arriving in Israel is scattered, outdated, or only available in Hebrew. GuiaOlim puts it all in one place, in Spanish, written by someone who went through the process.
