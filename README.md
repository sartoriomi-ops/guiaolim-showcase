# GuiaOlim

**Practical Israel resource hub for Latin American olim.**

🌐 **Language:** Neutral Spanish (accessible across all Latin American countries)

---

## What it is

GuiaOlim is a content brand that helps Latin American immigrants navigate life in Israel. It covers the practical stuff nobody tells you — bureaucracy, banking, healthcare, housing, workplace culture — in clear, jargon-free Spanish.

## What it covers

- Bituach Leumi (social security), Kupat Cholim (healthcare), banking setup
- Housing search, rental contracts, arnona (municipal tax)
- Workplace culture, Hebrew for the office, job search strategies
- Community resources, ulpan tips, social integration

## Content channels

- **Instagram** — reels, carousels, and stories with practical tips
- **TikTok** — short-form video guides
- **HTML guides** — in-depth walkthroughs for complex processes

## Tech

| Layer | Tech |
|---|---|
| Content generation | Claude API + custom GuiaOlim skills |
| Automations | Make.com (scheduling, cross-posting) |
| Guides | Self-contained HTML files |
| Content pipeline | Instagram + TikTok publishing flow |

## Status

🔧 **In build** — content pipeline active, guides in development, social presence growing.

## Why I built it

I moved to Israel from Latin America and learned everything the hard way. GuiaOlim is the resource I wish I'd had — practical, in Spanish, and written by someone who actually went through the process.
